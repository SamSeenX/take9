About Take 9 Personality Assessment

Take 9 is a free, interactive personality test designed to explore psychological archetypes through abstract narrative scenarios. Unlike traditional Myers-Briggs (MBTI) or Enneagram tests, Take 9 uses an experiential "choose your own adventure" format to determine your alignment.

Available Modules: The Void (Cyberpunk Identity), The Castle (Hogwarts House Sorting), The Kingdom (Game of Thrones Archetypes), The Galaxy (Star Wars Personality), The Wild (Spirit Animal), The Villain (Antagonist Alignment), The Aesthetic (Core/Vibe Check), The Alignment (D&D Moral Compass), and The Era (Retro Tech Nostalgia).
